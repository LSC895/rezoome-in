# Rezoome Chrome Extension

A Chrome extension that integrates with the Rezoome platform to generate ATS-optimized resumes directly from job descriptions on any job portal.

## Features

- **One-Click Resume Generation**: Right-click on any job description and generate a tailored resume
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Works on LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Indeed, Naukri, Monster, and ZipRecruiter
- **Automatic Job Description Detection**: Smart extraction of job requirements from major job portals
- **Secure Authentication**: Connect with your Rezoome account for seamless integration
- **Master Resume Storage**: Upload and store your master resume in the extension
- **Instant Downloads**: Generated resumes are automatically downloaded

## Installation

1. Download the extension files
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder
5. The Rezoome extension icon will appear in your toolbar

## Setup

1. Click the Rezoome extension icon in your toolbar
2. Click "Connect to Rezoome" to authenticate with your account
3. Upload your master resume through the extension popup
4. You're ready to generate tailored resumes!

## How to Use

1. **Navigate to any job posting** on supported platforms
2. **Select the job description text** you want to use
3. **Right-click** and choose "Generate Resume with Rezoome"
4. **Wait for generation** - you'll see a notification
5. **Download your tailored resume** automatically

## Privacy & Security

- All data is processed securely through the Rezoome platform
- Your master resume is stored encrypted in your browser
- Authentication tokens are stored locally and encrypted
- No job portal data is stored permanently

## Troubleshooting

**Extension not working?**
- Ensure you're logged in to your Rezoome account
- Check that you've uploaded a master resume
- Verify the job portal is supported

**Can't extract job description?**
- Try selecting the job description text manually
- Ensure you're on a job posting page, not a search results page
- Refresh the page and try again

**Authentication issues?**
- Clear extension data and reconnect your account
- Ensure popup blockers aren't preventing authentication
